My Journey

Becoming a programmer

Ever since I began programming, it has helped me in many ways.

Programming is mostly about logic. Every program or application that you create, is simply a logic puzzle.

'How do I tell the computer what I want it to do?' Syntax, and logic are the biggest thing to learning any progarmming language.

Because of my passion for programming, it has greatly changed the way I view problems, and also imrpoved my problem solving skills

In short, programming > teaching English

Chase your dreams

Programming was not actually my first choice for what I wanted to do.

When I began going to college, I wanted to become an english teacher. I soon discovered that I did not enjoy research papers.

So I began looking for something else to do as a future career. Something that would 'pay the bills' so to speak.

I then ran into programming. I thought I would give it a try. And to my pleasant surprise, I loved it!
